About Travel Kutaisi

Travel Kutaisi was established more than 10 years ago. We have started with advising the city visitors on popular destinations and convenient accommodation packages. The guests’ positive feedback and high evaluation of our service encouraged us to add tour management to our main activities. We partner with international Travel Agencies like “Viator”, “GetYourGuide”, “Expedia”, “Like a local Guide” featuring our service as excellent in almost every trip.

With Tours from Kutaisi we cover almost every region of Georgia: Kutaisi Area, Seaside Adjara Batumi, Svaneti – Mestia, Akhaltsikhe – Vardzia, Tbilisi the Capital, Kakheti – wine region, Khevsureti and Tusheti. Travel Kutaisi offers a full spectrum of incoming tourism services with flexible and efficient solutions, as a one stop supplier in Georgia. There is a large variety of holidaying and travelling options, which range from restful to recreational, from hiking to family adventure tours.

While planning Tours in Georgia it’s our policy and work-style to provide high quality personalized and warm-hearted services to all our guests – from the initial enquiry stage to the completion of the tour. Perfect knowledge of Georgia, long-term experience enables us to offer you the very best of the travel guidance.
