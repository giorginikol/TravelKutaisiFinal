Kutaisi City Tour



Join Kutaisi City Tour and visit most spectacular sights of ancient capital. A tour begins with a walk through old streets of Kutaisi downtown. After an hour of sightseeing, climb aboard comfortable tour bus. We head to villages surrounding city of Kutaisi. First, stop at the Gelati Monastery, built by the most powerful Georgian King David the Builder. Marvel at architectural masterpiece of middle ages and unique mosaic frescos. Visit the grave of the King and know about his last will left for generations. Listen to legends and stories about heroes defending their homeland .

Kutaisi City Tour program includes visit to Motsameta Monastery, which offers awe-inspiring views of the river gorge Tskaltsitela. It is named after the brothers Davit and Konstantin Mkheidze, tortured by Arabs for defending Christianity. Bagrati Cathedral, place of crowning Georgian Kings, will host you with its own story and an impressive panoramic views over the city. Discover unforgettable beauty of Prometheus Cave and feel like a hero on a mysterious adventure when riding a boat across the underground lake. At the end let’s feel and enjoy unique atmosphere of once famous Soviet Sanatoriums. They are located in the Town of Tskaltubo, beloved place of Soviet Leaders. It’s posible to book half day tour !
